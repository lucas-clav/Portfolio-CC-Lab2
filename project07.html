Good Things Come in Threes

Good Things Come in Threes is an autobiographical game where the player assumes the role of a chef tasked with creating a symbolic, three-course menu for a restaurant. Each culinary course represents a distinct stage of my life: an appetizer for my childhood, a main course for my adulthood, and a dessert representing my future self.
