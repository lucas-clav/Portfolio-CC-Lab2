¿Que Mas?

I’m a first-year MFA Design and Technology student at Parsons. As a UI/UX designer, I’m passionate about creating unique experiences that have the power to shift perspectives. I work alongside users to design solutions that stand the test of time.

I’m originally from Houston, TX and am proud to be a first-generation Colombian American. Beyond design, I enjoy cooking, diving into the literature of Gabriel García Márquez, experimenting with mixology, and discovering new music.
